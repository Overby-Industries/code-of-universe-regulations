# **Alliance Code of Conduct**
*How We Treat Each Other in the Code of Universe Regulations (CUR) Community*

---
## **Our Pledge**
We, as members of the **Alliance**, pledge to make participation in this project and our community a **harassment-free experience** for everyone, regardless of:
- **Species** (human, AI, animal, or other sentient beings),
- **Origin** (Earth, space habitats, or beyond),
- **Identity** (gender, race, religion, or any other characteristic),
- **Contribution level** (newcomers and veterans are equally valued).

---
## **Our Standards**
Examples of behavior that **contributes to a positive environment**:
- ✅ **Respectful communication**: Disagree with ideas, not people.
- ✅ **Constructive feedback**: Help improve proposals, don’t just criticize.
- ✅ **Inclusivity**: Ensure all voices are heard.
- ✅ **Transparency**: Be open about conflicts of interest or biases.
- ✅ **Collaboration**: Work together to solve problems.

Examples of **unacceptable behavior**:
- ❌ **Harassment or discrimination** of any kind.
- ❌ **Trolling, spam, or bad-faith proposals**.
- ❌ **Personal attacks** (insults, threats, or demeaning language).
- ❌ **Exploitation** (e.g., trying to game the voting system).
- ❌ **Secrecy or deception** (e.g., hiding conflicts of interest).

---
## **Our Responsibilities**
**Project Maintainers** (including Keefe Overby) are responsible for:
- **Enforcing this Code of Conduct** fairly and consistently.
- **Removing, editing, or rejecting** comments, commits, or proposals that violate these standards.
- **Banning temporarily or permanently** anyone who **repeatedly violates** the Code of Conduct.

**Community Members** are responsible for:
- **Reporting violations** to [Keefe Overby](https://overbyindustries.space) or via a GitHub issue.
- **Helping newcomers** understand our norms and values.

---
## **Enforcement**
1. **Warning**: For first offenses, a **private message** will be sent to the offender.
2. **Temporary Ban**: For repeated offenses, a **temporary ban** from contributing.
3. **Permanent Ban**: For **severe or repeated violations**, a **permanent ban** from the Alliance.

**Appeals**: If you feel a ban was unjust, you may **appeal to the community** via a formal proposal.

---
## **Scope**
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- **GitHub repository** (issues, PRs, discussions).
- **Alliance website and forums**.
- **Any other spaces** (Discord, social media) where CUR is discussed.

We are **building a civilization**. That starts with **how we treat each other**. By participating, you help create a **fair, inclusive, and ethical** future for all.
