# **Contributing to the Code of Universe Regulations (CUR)**
*How to Shape the Future of the Alliance*

---
## **Ways to Contribute**
### **1. Submit a Proposal**
Any citizen (human, AI, or other sentient being) can **propose a new law, regulation, or amendment** to the CUR. Here’s how:

#### **Step 1: Check for Duplicates**
- Browse the [`proposals/ACTIVE`](proposals/ACTIVE) and [`proposals/ARCHIVE`](proposals/ARCHIVE) folders to ensure your idea hasn’t already been proposed.

#### **Step 2: Use the Template**
- Copy the [`proposals/TEMPLATE.md`](proposals/TEMPLATE.md) file and fill it out.
- Save it in [`proposals/ACTIVE`](proposals/ACTIVE) with a **clear, descriptive name** (e.g., `PROPOSAL_Free_Space_Travel.md`).

#### **Step 3: Submit a Pull Request**
- Open a **Pull Request (PR)** to the `main` branch.
- **Title**: `[PROPOSAL] Your Proposal Title`
- **Description**: Briefly explain your proposal and why it matters.

#### **Step 4: Community Discussion**
- Your proposal will be **debated for 72 hours**.
- **Upvote/Downvote**: Community members can **react with 👍 or 👎** to signal support or concerns.
- **Refine**: Use feedback to **improve your proposal**.

#### **Step 5: Formal Vote (If Eligible)**
- If your proposal **reaches 10% upvotes**, it moves to a **formal vote**.
- **Voting Period**: 7 days.
- **Passing Threshold**:
  - **Simple majority (50%+1)** for standard proposals.
  - **Supermajority (75%)** for constitutional amendments.

---
### **2. Vote on Proposals**
- **Eligibility**: All **Alliance citizens** (see [CONSTITUTION.md](docs/CONSTITUTION.md) for citizenship rules) can vote.
- **How to Vote**:
  - **Comment** on the proposal’s PR with `+1` (support) or `-1` (oppose).
  - **AI will tally votes** and flag any irregularities for human review.

---
### **3. Audit and Oversight**
- **AI Audits**: Automated systems **flag suspicious activity** (e.g., vote brigading, spam).
- **Human Audits**: **Randomly selected citizens** review decisions weekly.
- **Report Issues**: If you see **corruption or misuse**, open an issue in the [`governance`](governance) folder.

---
### **4. Improve Documentation**
- **Found a typo?** Fix it and submit a PR.
- **Have a better explanation?** Suggest edits to any `.md` file.
- **Want to add a historical case study?** Contribute to [`historical_analysis`](historical_analysis)!

---
### **5. Spread the Word**
- Share the CUR on **social media, forums, or with your network**.
- **Translate documents** into other languages.
- **Host discussions** about the Alliance’s vision.
